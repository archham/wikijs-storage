## Installation

``` bash
    1  wget https://www.cendio.com/downloads/server/tl-4.16.0-server.zip

    3  ll
    4  cd tl-4.16.0-server/
    5  ll
    6  ./install-server 
    7  sudo visudo
    8  exit
    9  history 
   10  vi /opt/thinlinc/sbin/tl-setup.
   11  vi /opt/thinlinc/sbin/tl-setup 
   12  history
```
